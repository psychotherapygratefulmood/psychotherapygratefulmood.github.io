How it works

Online meeting - what to expect?

  • Step 1

    Set your appointment here

    Check the schedule for available hours, pick a time and proceed to payment online. Schedule hours correspond to the UTC+1 Central European Time Zone! Make sure to provide the accurate contact information (e-mail/phone). Once the booking is confirmed, you will receive an e-mail from me. Alternatively, we can schedule a short online meeting for free, in order to get to know each other, before any booking; for that, send me an e-mail at info@gratefulmood.com

  • Step 2

    Setup

    Make sure to have Skype/Vcall/Signal/E-mail (according to your preference) installed on your device and connect at least 10 minutes before the scheduled meeting. Check my article below about how to choose the best communication platform, that protects your data and privacy!

  • Step 3

    First meeting

    Our first meeting will be slightly different than the following ones; the intial contact with a therapist might provoke anxiety or anticipation distress, so in the first part of the meeting, we will focus on the boundaries and style of our collaboration. The sooner we overcome the initial communication obstacles, the more you will notice the benefits of the therapy. We will also discuss about consent, privacy and data protection. Further, I will ask you a set of questions, in order to establish the approach that is suited for you. In the second part of the meeting we'll focus on your current concerns.

  • Step 4

    Followup

    If we decide to go on with the therapy, we will start a deeper exploration of your feelings, thoughts and behaviors, in order to get a more profound understanding of how your mind operates and to improve your current situation. This can involve different types of exercises, practical talks, homeworks, simulations, etc. The recommended followup is one meeting/week, during minimum 12 weeks. If you decide to sign up for a 12 weeks followup, you will get a 1 session for free.
