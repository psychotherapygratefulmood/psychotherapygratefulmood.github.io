How it works

Online - what to expect?

  • Step 1

    Set your appointment here

    Check the schedule for available hours, pick a time and proceed to payment online. Make sure to provide the accurate contact information (e-mail/phone). Once the booking is confirmed, you will receive an e-mail from me.

  • Step 2

    Setup

    Make sure to install Skype, Vcall, Signal, E-mail - according to your booking - and connect at least 10 minutes before the scheduled meeting. Check my article below about how to choose the best communication platform, that protects your data and privacy!

  • Step 3

    First meeting

    Your intial contact with a therapist might provoke anxiety or anticipation distress; in the first part of the meeting, we will focus on the boundaries and style of our collaboration. The sooner we overcome the initial communication obstacles, the more you will access the benefits of the therapy. We will also discuss about consent, privacy and data protection. Further, I will ask you a set of questions, in order to establish the approach that is suited for you. In the second part of the meeting we'll focus on your current concerns or burdens.

  • Step 4

    Followup

    If the therapy suits you, we will further apply the adequate techniques and approaches, according to your needs in order to improve your interpersonal relations, functionality or performance. This can consist of exercices, practices, exploration of the feelings, behaviour or thoughts, homeworks, etc. The recommended followup is one meeting/week, during minimum 12 weeks. If you decide to sign up for a 12 weeks followup, you will get a 1 session for free.
