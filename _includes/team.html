I am a Medical Doctor, specializing in Psychiatry & Psychotherapy. I hold a Medical Diploma delivered by the University of Medicine and Pharmacy "Iuliu Hatieganu" Cluj-Napoca. I started being interested in mental health and psychiatry from my early years as a medical student, attending psychiatry, psychology and self-help meetups and organising the monthly Psychiatry scientific communication meetup, for one year, in my university.

I studied in Romania and France and I'm currently based in Prague, Czech Republic.

My experience in Psychiatry (Clinical University Hospital of Cluj-Napoca, Romania and EPSM Roche-sur-Foron, France), under the guidance of university professors and assistants, offers me the ability to recognize mental health disorders, offer assistance and guidance and therapy followups.

My current focuses are Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, DBT - Dialectical behavior therapy, Medical Hypnosis and Lucid dreaming techniques. Check out my notes below.

Of course, we can schedule a first online meeting for free. Contact me at info@gratefulmood.com and we will set an appointment. Then, you can describe me your problem, I prepare a therapy approach for you and I explain to you the next steps of our collaboration.
